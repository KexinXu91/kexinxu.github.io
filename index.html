Work

Social Media Management & Graphic Design

I am proficient in designing trade show and campaign materials using Adobe Creative Suite, including flyers, posters, web banners, and social media images, to enhance brand visibility and attract attendees' attention.

Trade Show Coordination

During my last internship at ACROBiosystems, I served as their marketing intern responsible for coordinating trade show logistics. This encompassed various tasks, including proposing trade show plans for upper management's approval, scheduling meetings with the sales team and other departments before the trade show, coordinating travel plans for the team, and ensuring the smooth execution of the trade show.

Upadacitinib Launch Campaign

When I worked at HAVAS Group China as their PR Intern, I played a pivotal role in the success of the Upadacitinib launch campaign for AbbVie, which garnered over 2,150,000 impressions, 150 media coverages, and 69 media reposts. This was a comprehensive long-term campaign that included both online impressions and offline events at local pharmacies. I drafted press releases for the campaign, introducing the drug to the public and reaching hospitals.

Children's Motrin Campaign

Another campaign I pariticpated in is Children's Motrin Campaign for J&J. I evaluated and assessed the campaign feedback, then reported the result to the team for the improvement. The campaign has achieved more than 1.05 million impressions and surpassed 91.33% of browsing volume in the same category.

California Orange County Newborn Diseases News

For this news project, I interviewed five California newborn professionals to investigate common newborn diseases and educate the public on how to deal with them. I produced a captivating 2-minute short news video , engaging viewers and delivering complex information in a concise manner. This news report was later turned into a 20-minute talk show, transforming complex health issues into an engaging and informative program for viewers.
